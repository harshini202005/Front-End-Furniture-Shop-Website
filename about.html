In 2012, we started Comfy Furnitures with a vision - to make a million homes beautiful. Back then, our catalogue featured just 30 designs. Yet, when our customers shared photographs of their homes, we saw they were remarkably distinct. The same table, the same chair was used differently by different people. This told us something. People want their homes to be unique.


Today, we understand that we’re here not just to sell well-designed products. We’re here to help you create
spaces that mirror who you are. At Comfy Furnitures, we want you to discover the joy of creating. We want you to think of the setting up of a space as an energizing, creative pursuit.

Life can be a ‘bed of roses’ (if the bed is from Comfy Furnitures)
